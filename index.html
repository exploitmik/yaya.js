Yaya.js

A lightweight lib for light animations

Add attribute "data-yaya" in element for anime. Available types:

  • yayaClipToRight
  • yayaClipToLeft
  • yayaClipCircle
  • yayaToUp
  • yayaToDown
  • yayaToRight
  • yayaToLeft
  • yayaBlurOut
